1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They help the site recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

Cookies serve various purposes, such as enabling the website to function properly, analyzing website performance, remembering login details, and delivering personalized content or advertisements.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Each browser provides tools for controlling or deleting cookies:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored on your device.
  • Block cookies from specific sites.
  • Delete existing cookies automatically when closing the browser.
