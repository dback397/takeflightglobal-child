  • Analytics Cookies: Collect aggregated, anonymized data about site performance and usage (e.g., via Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Enable features such as remembering login sessions or previously filled form fields.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline or delete them. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

By continuing to use this website, you consent to the placement of cookies in accordance with this Policy. You may adjust your cookie preferences at any time through your browser or our consent banner.
